  • Heterogeneous Graph Attention Network #待读

  • Metadata

    • * Tags: #Computer-Science—Social-and-Information-Networks

    • * Authors: [[Xiao Wang]], [[Houye Ji]], [[Chuan Shi]], [[Bai Wang]], [[Peng Cui]], [[P. Yu]], [[Yanfang Ye]]

  • Abstract

Graph neural network, as a powerful graph representation technique based on deep learning, has shown superior performance and attracted considerable research interest. However, it has not been fully considered in graph neural network for heterogeneous graph which contains different types of nodes and links. The heterogeneity and rich semantic information bring great challenges for designing a graph neural network for heterogeneous graph. Recently, one of the most exciting advancements in deep learning is the attention mechanism, whose great potential has been well demonstrated in various areas. In this paper, we ﬁrst propose a novel heterogeneous graph neural network based on the hierarchical attention, including node-level and semantic-level attentions. Speciﬁcally, ==the node-level attention aims to learn the importance between a node and its metapath based neighbors==, while ==the semantic-level attention is able to learn the importance of different meta-paths==. With the learned importance from both node-level and semantic-level attention, the importance of node and meta-path can be fully considered. Then the proposed model can generate node embedding by aggregating features from meta-path based neighbors in a hierarchical manner. Extensive experimental results on three real-world heterogeneous graphs not only show the superior performance of our proposed model over the state-of-the-arts, but also demonstrate its potentially good interpretability for graph analysis.

  • 笔记

    节点注意力学习节点和基于元路径的邻居之间的重要性

    语义注意力学习不同元路径之间的重要性

    • 异构图（b）：一个异构图，表示为 $G=(V,E)$ ，由一个对象集 V 和连接集 E 组成。异类图还与节点类型映射函数 $\phi:V->A$和连接类型映射函数 $\psi:E->B$ 相关联。A和 B 表示预定义对象类型和连接类型的集合，其中$| A |+| B |>2$(节点对应的类别和边对应的类别之和要大于2)

    • 元路径（c）：连接两个对象之间的复合关系，不同元路径揭示不同语义（m1 ->a1 ->m2）在给定元路径的情况下，每个节点存在一组基于元路径的邻居，可以在异构图中显示不同的结构信息和丰富的语义

    • 基于元路径的邻居（d）：在元路径m1 ->a1 ->m2 中m1 和 m2 就是邻居关系，在（b）异构图中基于元路径m->a->m的邻居为：m1 m2 m3 (节点的邻居包括自身)

模型部分：

==节点注意力来学习基于元路径的邻居的权重==，对其进行聚合得到node embedding，在此基础上，通过==语义注意力学到元路径的差异==，得到特定任务的语义的embedding

  • 节点级注意力

    ——投影函数：将节点映射到同一维度（hi 是原始特征）

    ​ 之后利用自注意力学习各节点之间的权重

    ——基于元路径$\phi$节点对（i,j）j对i的重要性（==attention==）

    ​ 通过mask attention只计算$e_{ij}^{\phi}$,也就是i和基于元路径的邻居节点的注意力

    ——==attention系数==

    ——节点i基于元路径$\phi$学习到的embedding，

    ​ 由于异构图具有无标度特性，因此图数据的方差很大

    ——拓展到了多头注意力，就是将注意力重复k次再把得到的embedding拼接在一起

    故给定一个元路径集合${\phi_1,\phi_2,…,\phi_p}$,把节点特征输入给节点注意力，会得到p组语义节点embedding ${Z_{\phi_1},Z{\phi_2},…,Z{\phi_p}}$

  • 语义级注意力

    将节点级注意力学习到的p组语义节点embedding ${Z_{\phi_1},Z{\phi_2},…,Z{\phi_p}}$作为输入到语义级注意力attsem中，学习到每个元路径的权重$(\beta_{\phi_1},\beta_{\phi_2},…,\beta_{\phi_p})$

    $att_{sem}$的attention为:

    1. 首先通过一个非线性变换（MLP）变换语义节点embedding，
    2. 利用语义级注意力向量q与非线性变换后的embedding相似性（内积），来测量语义节点embedding的重要性
    3. 进一步平均了所有语义节点embedding的重要性，可以解释为每个元路径的重要性。故每个元路径的重要性，记为 $w_{\phi_i}$
    4. 最后通过softmax对其进行归一化(注意力系数)，可以解释为元路径$\phi_i$对特定任务的贡献
