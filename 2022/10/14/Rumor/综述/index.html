rumor综述

发表于2022-10-14|更新于2023-02-27|rumor
|阅读量:

“social media have become a critical publishing tool for journalists [31, 90] and the main consumption method for citizens looking for the latest news [43]. Journalists may use social media to report on public opinions about breaking news stories, and even to discover potential new stories, whereas citizens may follow the development of breaking news and events through official channels (i.e. news outlets official accounts on social media platforms) or through posts of their own network (e.g. friends, family, public figures).” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 2) (pdf) 社交媒体已成为记者的重要发布工具 [31, 90] 和公民寻找最新消息的主要消费方式 [43]。记者可以利用社交媒体报道公众对突发新闻的看法，甚至发现潜在的新故事，而公民可以通过官方渠道（即社交媒体平台上的新闻机构官方账号）或通过官方渠道关注突发新闻和事件的发展。他们自己网络上的帖子（例如朋友、家人、公众人物）。

“However, this positive impact of the social media comes at a cost: the absence of control and fact-checking over posts makes social media a fertile ground for the spread of unverified and/or false information [111]. People often publish posts or share other people’s posts verifying neither the source nor the information validity and reliability. Oftentimes, an attractive headline is sufficient for an article to be shared thousands of times, despite a possibly unsubstantiated or false content. For example, [101] reports that in 2016 a fake news about Hillary Clinton leading a child sex trafficking ring led a man to assault with a rifle a pizzeria that was claimed as one of the hubs of such trafficking [52]. This is only one example, but countless fake news and rumours are spread through social media every day for different reasons.” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 3) (pdf) 然而，社交媒体的这种积极影响是有代价的：缺乏对帖子的控制和事实核查使得社交媒体成为传播未经证实和/或虚假信息的沃土[111]。人们经常发布帖子或分享其他人的帖子，既不验证来源，也不验证信息的有效性和可靠性。通常，一个吸引人的标题足以让一篇文章被分享数千次，尽管可能存在未经证实或虚假的内容。例如，[101] 报道称，2016 年，一则关于希拉里·克林顿 (Hillary Clinton) 领导儿童性交易团伙的假新闻导致一名男子用步枪袭击了一家据称是此类贩运中心之一的比萨店 [52]。这只是一个例子，但每天都有无数的假新闻和谣言出于不同的原因通过社交媒体传播。

“Fake news are not, however, a product of the digital communication age. Before the advent of internet, journalists were tasked with verification and fact checking of news and sources [79]” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 3) (pdf) 然而，假新闻并不是数字传播时代的产物。在互联网出现之前，记者的任务是对新闻和消息来源进行核实和事实核查【79】从而使公众舆论对假新闻的曝光更加有限。

“Nowadays, social media facilitate the spread of the unverified and false information among a larger number of users, thus deeply influencing the global perception and the understanding of events [111].” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 3) (pdf) 如今，社交媒体促进了未经证实的虚假信息在大量用户中的传播，从而深刻影响了全球对事件的感知和理解[111]更广泛地说，虚假信息正在成为网络上的一个巨大问题，并可能在未来产生重要的社会成本。

“In studying rumour spread on Twitter, the authors in [91] noted that, within a conversation on a topic, users express opinions on its veracity, potentially giving insight to other users. Experimental results presented in [116], however, have shown that true rumours tend to be resolved faster than fake ones, and that unverified rumours produce a distinctive burst in the number of re-tweets within the first few minutes considerably higher than the one generated by rumours proven to be true or false. Furthermore, users generally tend to support unverified rumours. This, combined with the structure of social media platforms, may enable a chain reaction that can lead to the spread of unverified and potentially fake information.” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 4) (pdf) 在研究 Twitter 上传播的谣言时，[91] 中的作者指出，在关于某个主题的对话中，用户表达了对其真实性的看法，这可能会为其他用户提供洞察力。然而，[116] 中的实验结果已经表明，真实的谣言往往比虚假的谣言得到更快的解决，并且未经证实的谣言在最初几分钟内产生的转发数量明显激增，远高于被证明是真假的谣言所产生的数量。此外，用户通常倾向于支持未经证实的谣言。这与社交媒体平台的结构相结合，可能会引发连锁反应，从而导致未经证实的和潜在的虚假信息的传播。

“”Fake news” has become the de-facto expression for identifying false information in mainstream media, especially for web-related content, mostly spreading during and after the 2016 U.S. Presidential Campaign. However, research on fake news generally uses a more restrictive definition. Following [4], a fake news is “a news article that is intentionally and verifiably false”. Such definition hinges on two key aspects: intent and verifiability. Fake news are therefore news articles that are intentionally written to mislead or misinform readers, but can be verified as false by means of other sources. Several recent studies, such as [24], have adopted this definition.
-—
“假新闻”已成为在主流媒体中识别虚假信息的事实上的表达方式，尤其是与网络相关的内容，主要在 2016 年美国总统竞选期间和之后传播。然而，对假新闻的研究通常使用更严格的定义。在 [4] 之后，假新闻是“故意且可验证为虚假的新闻文章”。这种定义取决于两个关键方面：意图和可验证性。因此，假新闻是故意为误导或误导读者而撰写的新闻文章，但可以通过其他来源验证为假新闻。最近的几项研究，如[24]，已经采用了这个定义。” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 8) (pdf)

“In the recent scientific literature, rumours are probably the most widely studied false information on the web. They refer to information that has not been confirmed by official sources yet and is spread mostly by users on social media platforms.
-—
在最近的科学文献中，谣言可能是网络上研究最广泛的虚假信息。它们是指尚未得到官方消息来源确认的信息，主要由用户在社交媒体平台上传播。” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 9) (pdf)

“Rumours are not a product of the Internet age, with the early studies dating back to the end of the World War II [5, 6]. However, we can argue that Internet and social media platforms in particular are a fertile ground for the spread of unconfirmed information [97].
-—
谣言不是互联网时代的产物，早期的研究可以追溯到二战结束 [5, 6]。然而，我们可以说，互联网和社交媒体平台尤其是未经证实的信息传播的沃土[97]。” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 9) (pdf)

“To provide a formal definition of rumour is not straightforward. In fact, researchers have reported different interpretations. One of the most widely adopted definitions comes from the authors in [30]. In their research, rumours are identified as “unverified and instrumentally relevant information statements in circulation”. Furthermore, [115] defines a rumour more specifically as a “circulating story of questionable veracity, which is apparently credible but hard to verify, and produces sufficient skepticism and/or anxiety”. For this definition a rumour has to produce an impactful reaction on its audience. However, we can argue that these definitions hinge on the “unverified” characteristic of the information. This unverified information could be true, partly true, entirely false or remain unverified [111].
-—
为谣言提供一个正式的定义并不简单。事实上，研究人员报告了不同的解释。最广泛采用的定义之一来自 [30] 中的作者。在他们的研究中，谣言被确定为“流通中未经证实且与工具相关的信息声明”。此外，[115] 将谣言更具体地定义为“流传的真实性有问题的故事，显然可信但难以核实，并产生足够的怀疑和/或焦虑”。对于这个定义，谣言必须对其受众产生有影响力的反应。然而，我们可以争辩说，这些定义取决于信息的“未经证实”的特征。这些未经证实的信息可能是真实的、部分真实的、完全虚假的或未经证实的 [111]。” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 9) (pdf)

在收集假新闻的过程中，要考虑的一个关键方面是如何进行事实核查，以获得可靠的假新闻和真新闻数据集。在 [85] 中，作者概述了在基于知识的假新闻建模和识别的背景下现有的事实检查方法。面向专家的事实核查依赖于人类专家来评估新闻的真实性。例如，Snopes8 和 FactCheck.org9 等网站利用了此过程。面向专家的事实检查的主要问题是时间和资源成本。一种可能的替代方法是在事实检查过程中实施众包，以利用“群众的智慧”来注释潜在的可疑新闻内容，例如在 Fiskkit10 等服务中。最后，[85] 提到了面向计算的事实检查模型，该模型通常基于算法和外部资源（例如知识图和开放网络）来评估值得检查的新闻及其真实性。特别是，可以利用面向专家和面向众包的事实检查来可靠地注释假新闻数据集。

谣言主要在社交媒体上进行研究。这是因为社交媒体平台通常用于在用户之间尽快共享信息。这可能会导致共享未经验证的信息，进而可能传播并产生谣言。

“The literature on how datasets should be collected for rumours detection and analysis mostly focuses on two main strategies, namely top-down and bottom-up collection strategies [111]. Top-down strategy requires some form of a-priori knowledge about target rumours. In particular, rumours are usually collected, after they spread on social media, by searching for a specific set of keywords and tags that describe the rumour. The proposed strategy is quite straightforward to implement and thus has been employed in several researches related to rumour detection and verification [15, 64, 75, 97, 109]. In this context rumour debunking websites are often used as source to identify the most interesting rumours and to extract reliable keywords for retrieving posts about those rumours on social media [22, 51, 57, 62, 63, 75].
-—
关于如何收集数据集以进行谣言检测和分析的文献主要集中在两种主要策略上，即自上而下和自下而上的收集策略[111]。自上而下的策略需要某种形式的关于目标谣言的先验知识。特别是，谣言在社交媒体上传播后，通常通过搜索描述谣言的一组特定关键字和标签来收集。所提出的策略实施起来非常简单，因此已被用于与谣言检测和验证相关的多项研究中 [15, 64, 75, 97, 109]。在这种情况下，谣言揭穿网站通常被用作识别最有趣的谣言并提取可靠关键字以在社交媒体上检索有关这些谣言的帖子的来源[22、51、57、62、63、75]。” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 14) (pdf)

“Many subtasks in rumour analysis have been suggested, namely rumour detection, rumour veracity classification, rumour stance classification, and rumour tracking among others [111].
-—
已经提出了谣言分析中的许多子任务，即谣言检测、谣言真实性分类、谣言立场分类和谣言跟踪等[111]。” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 15) (pdf)

“Rumour detection is the first step in a rumour classification system [111].
-—
谣言检测是谣言分类系统的第一步[111]。” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 15) (pdf)

“Rumour veracity classification can be considered as the other major subtask in rumour analysis, and has been extensively studied from various perspectives
-—
谣言真实性分类可以被认为是谣言分析中的另一个主要子任务，并且已经从各个角度进行了广泛的研究” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 15) (pdf)

“rumours have to be known a-priori, making such datasets not suitable for the discovery of breaking news type of rumours,” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 14) (pdf) 谣言必须先验地知道，使得此类数据集不适合发现突发新闻类型的谣言（自上而下的缺点）

“Stance classification concerns instead the identification of opinions in social media posts with respect to a given rumour: the classification has been performed at the beginning as a 2class problem (support and deny) [75], or more recently as a 4-class problem (support,deny, question, and comment) [74, 116]. Data for this task have been collected both using a top-down [74, 75], and a bottom-up strategy [113].
-—
立场分类关注的是社交媒体帖子中关于给定谣言的意见的识别：分类一开始是作为 2 类问题（支持和否认）[75]，或者最近作为 4 类问题（支持、否认、问题和评论）[74, 116]。已使用自上而下 [74, 75] 和自下而上策略 [113] 收集了此任务的数据。” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 16) (pdf)

“Stance classification concerns instead the identification of opinions in social media posts with respect to a given rumour: the classification has been performed at the beginning as a 2class problem (support and deny) [75], or more recently as a 4-class problem (support,deny, question, and comment) [74, 116]. Data for this task have been collected both using a top-down [74, 75], and a bottom-up strategy [113]” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 16) (pdf) 立场分类关注的是社交媒体帖子中关于给定谣言的意见的识别：分类一开始是作为 2 类问题（支持和否认）[75]，或者最近作为 4 类问题（支持、否认、问题和评论）[74, 116]。已使用自上而下 [74, 75] 和自下而上策略 [113] 收集了此任务的数据

谣言的数据集：“PHEME project [28]” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 18) (pdf)

内容特征：

“A large body of work in linguistics and NLP exists concerning the identification of linguistic cues for detection. Earliest studies on computer mediated communication [13, 110] had already shown promising results. Moreover, works specifically focused on social media communication [12, 72, 108] have been able to isolate various linguistic cues which characterize deception, such as the use of self reference, negative words, swear words. The most recent studies have focused on both linguistic categories signaling credibility and specific phrases [69]. These linguistic cues, in addition to other features, have been successfully implemented in numerous approaches concerning fake news and rumour detection [15, 38, 39, 63, 76, 78, 95, 108].” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 20) (pdf) 语言学和 NLP 方面的大量工作涉及识别语言线索以进行检测。最早的关于计算机中介通信的研究 [13, 110] 已经显示出有希望的结果。此外，专门针对社交媒体交流的作品 [12, 72, 108] 已经能够分离出具有欺骗特征的各种语言线索，例如使用自我指称、负面词、脏话。最近的研究集中在表示可信度的语言类别和特定短语[69]。除了其他特征外，这些语言提示已在许多有关假新闻和谣言检测的方法中成功实施 [15, 38, 39, 63, 76, 78, 95, 108]。

“We can distinguish among syntactic, lexical and semantic linguistic features. Syntactic features, such as the number of content words (nouns, verbs, adjective), and the presence and frequency of specific POS patterns, have been employed, for instance, in [75, 76, 113]. In addition, sentence complexity has been identified as an indication for the reliability of the information [12, 97] as well as index of subjectivity [22, 40].” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 20) (pdf) 我们可以区分句法、词汇和语义语言特征。例如，在 [75, 76, 113] 中已经采用了句法特征，例如实词（名词、动词、形容词）的数量以及特定 POS 模式的存在和频率。此外，句子复杂性已被确定为信息可靠性的指标 [12, 97] 以及主观性指标 [22, 40]。

“Lexical features concern actual word usage in texts. In particular, the most straightforward approach is to exploit the most salient content words or expressions (e.g. bi-grams and tri-grams) as features [16, 34, 73, 78]. Moreover, it has been proven that negations, doubt words, abbreviations and vulgar expressions” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 20) (pdf) 词汇特征涉及文本中的实际单词使用。特别是，最直接的方法是利用最显着的内容词或表达（例如二元组和三元组）作为特征[16、34、73、78]。此外，已证明否定词、疑问词、缩写词和粗俗表达

“Semantic features are often extracted by means of advanced NLP techniques. For example, sentiment analysis and opinion mining approaches adopt features based on the opinions and emotions expressed in the text [15, 39, 75, 105]. In addition, topics of social media posts, extracted by means of Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA) [7], have been proposed as features by [48]. Distributional semantics techniques such as word embeddings [67] have proven to be useful in numerous contexts throughout the NLP research field, and have been successfully implemented also for fake news and rumours detection, especially in machine learning and deep learning approaches [51, 63, 81, 113].” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 21) (pdf) 语义特征通常是通过先进的 NLP 技术提取的。例如，情感分析和意见挖掘方法采用基于文本中表达的意见和情感的特征[15,39,75,105]。此外，通过潜在狄利克雷分配（LDA）[7]提取的社交媒体帖子主题已被[48]提出作为特征。诸如词嵌入 [67] 之类的分布式语义技术已被证明在整个 NLP 研究领域的众多上下文中都很有用，并且也已成功用于假新闻和谣言检测，特别是在机器学习和深度学习方法中 [51、63、 81, 113]。

“Content features have been extensively studied and implemented for fake news detection. Clearly, the content plays a key role in discovering potential deception. However, such features present some downsides. First, textual cues to deception may achieve limited generalization capability in a real word application system. Second, they may lose in descriptiveness as fake news are becoming more and more similar to proper news for what concerns the writing style. Finally, especially concerning rumours on social media, given the relative brevity of texts, other characteristics may prove to be more effective.” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 21) (pdf) 内容特征已被广泛研究并用于假新闻检测。显然，内容在发现潜在欺骗方面起着关键作用。但是，这些功能存在一些缺点。首先，欺骗的文本提示可能在真实单词应用系统中实现有限的泛化能力。其次，它们可能会失去描述性，因为假新闻在写作风格方面变得越来越类似于真正的新闻。最后，特别是关于社交媒体上的谣言，鉴于文本相对简洁，其他特征可能被证明更有效。

上下文特征

“Context features are extracted by considering relevant information surrounding the actual social media post or fake news. In particular, the most used context features concern the analysis of users, sources of the rumour or news, propagation structures of the information on social media, and reaction of other users with respect to the news.” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 21) (pdf) 通过考虑围绕实际社交媒体帖子或假新闻的相关信息来提取上下文特征。特别是，最常用的上下文特征涉及用户分析、谣言或新闻的来源、社交媒体上信息的传播结构以及其他用户对新闻的反应。

“User-based features model characteristics of social media users (e.g., number of posts, age of the account, number of friends/followers, etc.) who produce or share the news. Earliest studies on user credibility [15] have adopted some features directly extracted from information on the users in Twitter, obtaining promising results.” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 21) (pdf) 基于用户的特征：模型生成或分享新闻的社交媒体用户的特征（例如，帖子数量、帐户年龄、朋友/关注者数量等）。最早关于用户可信度的研究 [15] 采用了直接从 Twitter 用户信息中提取的一些特征，取得了可喜的结果。“the numbers of followers and followings (or a follower to following ratio) [57, 113” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 22) (pdf) 追随者和追随者的数量（或追随者与追随者的比率）[57, 113]“Moreover, many studies consider the age of the account on the platform, the presence of a clear description for the profile and of URLs linking to external resources [15, 17, 62, 64, 103, 105, 113].” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 22) (pdf) 此外，许多研究考虑了平台上帐户的年龄、个人资料的清晰描述以及链接到外部资源的 URL [15、17、62、64、103、105、113]。

“Network-based features are used to model information concerning the properties of the network where the news is shared. For example, propagation structures, diffusion patterns, and properties of the sub-graph in which the news is spread, such as density and clustering coefficient, are taken into account” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 22) (pdf)

基于网络的特征：用于对有关共享新闻的网络属性的信息进行建模。例如，考虑新闻传播的子图的传播结构、传播模式和属性，如密度和聚类系数“Finally, other works have incorporated both content and user based features as well as information from the propagation tree” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 22) (pdf) 最后，其他作品结合了内容和基于用户的特征以及来自传播树的信息“directly evaluate the similarity of propagation trees in order to infer the veracity of the source post.” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 23) (pdf) 直接评估传播树的相似性，以推断源帖子的真实性。

“In addition, some approaches have focused on exploiting the wisdom of the crowds to identify rumours and fake news. Such approaches in fact mostly implement the stance of other users in a platform towards a given rumour as features to determine its veracity. [116] has shown how an aggregate stance (e.g., mostly positive or mostly negative) may correspond to the veracity of a rumour. Albeit stance detection approaches have been proposed in the literature [29, 39, 75, 113], not many rumour or fake news detection systems, which employ such stance as feature, exist. [50], following [92], has exploited topic models [7] to identify conflicting viewpoints in microblogs, and has built a credibility network to determine the veracity of social media posts. [88] has used instead Facebook likes (i.e. users who like news posts) as features for classifying posts as either hoax or non hoax” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 23) (pdf) 此外，一些方法侧重于利用群众的智慧来识别谣言和假新闻。事实上，这些方法主要实现了平台中其他用户对给定谣言的立场，作为确定其真实性的特征。 [116] 展示了总体立场（例如，主要是积极的或主要是消极的）如何与谣言的真实性相对应。尽管文献 [29, 39, 75, 113] 中提出了立场检测方法，但使用这种立场作为特征的谣言或假新闻检测系统并不多见。 [50] 继 [92] 之后，利用主题模型 [7] 来识别微博中的冲突观点，并建立了一个可信度网络来确定社交媒体帖子的真实性。 [88] 使用 Facebook 的点赞（即喜欢新闻帖子的用户）作为将帖子分类为恶作剧或非恶作剧的特征

“Machine learning algorithms have proven to be extremely useful for solving numerous tasks in the information engineering field. Since the earliest approaches focusing on credibility on social media [15] and deception detection in computer mediated communication [108] had provided promising results, machine learning techniques have been implemented in a number of researches concerning the problem of false information detection on the web.” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 24) (pdf) 机器学习算法已被证明对于解决信息工程领域的众多任务非常有用。由于最早关注社交媒体可信度 [15] 和计算机中介通信中的欺骗检测 [108] 的方法提供了有希望的结果，因此机器学习技术已在许多关于网络上虚假信息检测问题的研究中得到应用。

“Support Vector Machines (SVMs) are one of the most widely used methods for classification in a number of research areas. SVMs are discriminative classifiers formally defined by a separating hyperplane” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 25) (pdf) 支持向量机 (SVM) 是许多研究领域中使用最广泛的分类方法之一。 SVM 是由分离超平面正式定义的判别分类器

“According to the experiments in [108], SVMs have outperformed a number of supervised machine learning approaches for deception detection in text, obtaining an F-measure F1 of 0.84. However, as pointed out by the authors themselves, there exists a significant variation in performance depending on the dataset selected for training [108].” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 25) (pdf) 根据 [108] 中的实验，支持向量机在文本欺骗检测方面的表现优于许多监督机器学习方法，获得的 F-measure F1 为 0.84。然而，正如作者自己所指出的，性能存在显着差异，具体取决于选择用于训练的数据集 [108]。

机器学习方法

“Machine learning algorithms have proven to be extremely useful for solving numerous tasks in the information engineering field. Since the earliest approaches focusing on credibility on social media [15] and deception detection in computer mediated communication [108] had provided promising results, machine learning techniques have been implemented in a number of researches concerning the problem of false information detection on the web.” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 24) (pdf) 机器学习算法已被证明对于解决信息工程领域的众多任务非常有用。由于最早关注社交媒体可信度 [15] 和计算机中介通信中的欺骗检测 [108] 的方法提供了有希望的结果，因此机器学习技术已在许多关于网络上虚假信息检测问题的研究中得到应用。

“Support Vector Machines (SVMs) are one of the most widely used methods for classification in a number of research areas. SVMs are discriminative classifiers formally defined by a separating hyperplane” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 25) (pdf) 支持向量机 (SVM) 是许多研究领域中使用最广泛的分类方法之一。 SVM 是由分离超平面正式定义的判别分类器

“According to the experiments in [108], SVMs have outperformed a number of supervised machine learning approaches for deception detection in text, obtaining an F-measure F1 of 0.84. However, as pointed out by the authors themselves, there exists a significant variation in performance depending on the dataset selected for training [108].” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 25) (pdf) 根据 [108] 中的实验，支持向量机在文本欺骗检测方面的表现优于许多监督机器学习方法，获得的 F-measure F1 为 0.84。然而，正如作者自己所指出的，性能存在显着差异，具体取决于选择用于训练的数据集 [108]。

“Another widely studied family of algorithms, proposed particularly for rumour analysis tasks, is decision tree [11]. Decision tree performs a recursive split on feature values in order to determine the class. Decision trees are generated from data with algorithms such as J48 (C4.5) [77]. Despite their relative simplicity with respect to other machine learning schemes, they exhibit competitive performance on the task at hand” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 26) (pdf) 另一个广泛研究的算法家族，特别是针对谣言分析任务提出的，是决策树[11]。决策树对特征值执行递归拆分以确定类别。决策树是使用 J48 (C4.5) [77] 等算法从数据中生成的。尽管它们相对于其他机器学习方案相对简单，但它们在手头的任务上表现出有竞争力的表现

“The effectiveness of J48 decision tree with respect to other algorithms, including SVM, has been shown in [3, 15, 36, 109]. More specifically, in [15] the authors have used a mixture of content-based and context-based features to perform credibility evaluation of tweets, achieving an accuracy of 0.86. In [8] the authors have proposed a series of user trust metrics in order to evaluate the trustworthiness of users in social media via decision tree. They have reported an accuracy of 0.75. Enquiring tweets were exploited in [109] in order to determine clusters of potential rumours. For each cluster, a series of features describing it are extracted and fed to a J48 decision tree. Authors report an accuracy of 0.52 for their best run. [36] has implemented a decision tree for veracity classification of rumours. The approach is able to achieve 0.96 accuracy on the proposed dataset, with a small number of features.” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 26) (pdf) J48 决策树相对于其他算法（包括 SVM）的有效性已在 [3, 15, 36, 109] 中展示。更具体地说，在 [15] 中，作者混合使用了基于内容和基于上下文的特征来对推文进行可信度评估，达到 0.86 的准确度。在 [8] 中，作者提出了一系列用户信任指标，以通过决策树评估社交媒体中用户的可信度。他们报告的准确度为 0.75。 [109] 中利用查询推文来确定潜在谣言的集群。对于每个集群，提取一系列描述它的特征并将其馈送到 J48 决策树。作者报告其最佳运行的准确度为 0.52。 [36] 已经实现了用于谣言真实性分类的决策树。该方法能够在所提出的数据集上实现 0.96 的准确度，并且具有少量特征。

“Random forests [10] have been employed in a number of works on rumour analysis. A random forest is an ensemble of several decision trees. The mode of all the predictions from each tree is the final output. Comparative studies on various machine learning algorithms for rumours and fake news tasks have outlined random forests as a strong performer [3, 12, 36, 57, 107]. More specifically, [57] has implemented a random forest with a set of temporal, structural and linguistic features for rumour classification in a tweet graph, obtaining an accuracy of 0.90. Random forest is also exploited for stance detection in [3, 107]. [107] has used only content-based features of tweets and has obtained an aver” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 26) (pdf) 随机森林 [10] 已被用于许多谣言分析工作中。随机森林是几个决策树的集合。来自每棵树的所有预测的模式是最终输出。针对谣言和假新闻任务的各种机器学习算法的比较研究表明，随机森林表现出色[3、12、36、57、107]。更具体地说，[57] 已经实现了一个随机森林，它具有一组时间、结构和语言特征，用于推文图中的谣言分类，准确度为 0.90。在 [3, 107] 中，随机森林也被用于立场检测。 [107] 仅使用了基于内容的推文特征，并获得平均准确度在 0.83 到 0.88 之间。在 [3] 中，作者提出了使用从以前的作品 [39, 62, 75, 116] 中派生的一组特征以及一组特定于立场的特征。他们在 RumorEval 数据集 [29] 上报告了 0.79 的准确度。在[48]中，随机森林也被用于执行用户可信度评估。作者已经提议扩展传统特征集以进行用户可信度评估，如在 [15] 中，通过 Latent Dirchlet 分配 (LDA) [7] 提取基于主题模型的特征。

“Conditional Random Field (CRF) classifiers [58] have received less attention for solving tasks in this domain, despite their relative popularity in NLP tasks such as for example PoS-Tagging. The main advantage of a CRF classifier is the capability of modelling sequences. [113, 114] has argued that a CRF is able to leverage previous context in order to improve the detection of rumors for breaking news events. Authors have reported an F1 of 0.6 on the PHMEME dataset [113, 116]. Results have shown that CRF outperforms non-sequential approaches as well.” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 27) (pdf) 条件随机场（CRF）分类器[58]在解决该领域的任务方面受到的关注较少，尽管它们在 NLP 任务（例如 PoS 标记）中相对受欢迎。 CRF 分类器的主要优点是能够对序列进行建模。 [113, 114] 认为 CRF 能够利用先前的上下文来改进对突发新闻事件谣言的检测。作者报告了 PHMEME 数据集 [113, 116] 上的 F1 为 0.6。结果表明，CRF 也优于非顺序方法。

“Hidden Markov Models (HMMs) are capable of dealing with sequence-based data too. HMMs have been exploited for rumour veracity classification in [96, 97].” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 27) (pdf) 隐马尔可夫模型 (HMM) 也能够处理基于序列的数据。在 [96, 97] 中，HMM 已被用于谣言真实性分类。

“Finally, an approach based on ensemble of classifiers has been implemented in [98]. Ensembles rely on the predictions of a number of independent classifiers, and provide the final classification via a majority vote scheme. [101] has proposed the use of a series of content- and context-based features fed to several learning algorithms. The ensemble is able to achieve 0.77 accuracy on the RumourEval test dataset [29].” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 28) (pdf) 最后，在[98]中实现了一种基于分类器集成的方法。集成依赖于多个独立分类器的预测，并通过多数投票方案提供最终分类。 [101] 提出了使用一系列基于内容和上下文的特征，这些特征被馈送到几种学习算法中。该集成能够在 RumourEval 测试数据集 [29] 上达到 0.77 的准确度。（98：Rumour evaluation using effective features and supervised ensemble models）

深度学习方法

“Deep Learning is one of the most widely explored research topics in machine learning. Deep Learning classifiers have seen an unprecedented rise in popularity in recent years, due to extremely promising results in a number of research fields, including text mining and NLP. Deep learning frameworks have a main advantage over traditional machine learning approaches. Indeed, traditional machine learning representations are based on manually crafted features. The feature extraction task is time-consuming and may result in biased features [63]. This is a critical issue for tasks such as fake news and rumour detection, where the identification of relevant features for the analysis may pose an even greater challenge. On the other hand, deep learning frameworks can learn hidden representations from simpler inputs both in context and content variations [63]. The problem is therefore shifted from modeling relevant input features to modeling the network itself in a way that enables the task to be solved efficiently.” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 28) (pdf) 深度学习是机器学习中探索最广泛的研究课题之一。近年来，由于在文本挖掘和自然语言处理等多个研究领域取得了非常有希望的成果，深度学习分类器的受欢迎程度空前上升。与传统机器学习方法相比，深度学习框架具有主要优势。事实上，传统的机器学习表示是基于手工制作的特征。特征提取任务非常耗时，并且可能导致有偏差的特征[63]。这对于假新闻和谣言检测等任务来说是一个关键问题，其中识别相关特征以进行分析可能会带来更大的挑战。另一方面，深度学习框架可以从上下文和内容变化中的简单输入中学习隐藏表示[63]。因此，问题从对相关输入特征进行建模转移到以能够有效解决任务的方式对网络本身进行建模。

RNN：

RNN 是一类神经网络，其中节点按顺序相互连接以形成有向图。这种结构使得 RNN，尤其是长短期记忆（LSTM）网络[44]，对于建模顺序数据（例如人类语言）以及从不同信息源捕获相关特征特别有效[81]。在假新闻和谣言的背景下，最早采用 RNN 进行谣言检测的报道见 [63]，**[54]用的多任务这个好像不一样**（All-in-one: Multi-task learning for rumour verification.）

“In [112], different approaches based on sequential models for stance classification have been evaluated, namely Linear and Tree CRF, Hawkes Process, and LSTM. In particular, authors have exploited features extracted from the sequential interactions of users on Twitter to classify the stance of each tweet.” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 29) (pdf) 在 [112] 中，评估了基于序列模型的姿态分类的不同方法，即线性和树 CRF、霍克斯过程和 LSTM。特别是，作者利用从 Twitter 上用户的顺序交互中提取的特征来对每条推文的立场进行分类。

CNN：

“CNNs are a class of neural networks with an input layer, an output layer, and a series of hidden layers, where a number of transformations are applied to the data by means of pooling and convolution operations. CNNs have been widely studied for image recognition and processing [60] and are considered the state-ofthe-art for many computer vision tasks. However, CNNs are gaining popularity in the NLP community as well [49]. Considering rumours and fake news, a number of recent works include CNNs.” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 29) (pdf) CNN 是一类具有输入层、输出层和一系列隐藏层的神经网络，其中通过池化和卷积操作对数据进行许多转换。 CNN 已被广泛研究用于图像识别和处理 [60]，并被认为是许多计算机视觉任务的最新技术。然而，CNN 在 NLP 社区中也越来越受欢迎 [49]。考虑到谣言和假新闻，最近的一些作品包括 CNN。

[20] 提出了一个单和多词embedding的 CNN,用于解决推文的立场和真实性分类。作者报告称，在 RumorEval 评估活动 [29] 中，姿态分类任务的准确度为 0.70，真实性分类任务的准确度为 0.53。这个可以看一下

“In addition, a number of recent works have exploited a mixture of RNNs and CNNs in their models [2, 87, 101]. [101] has proposed a hybrid approach to fake news detection on the LIAR dataset [101]. The proposed architecture encodes text information via a CNN, and metadata about the author of the text by means of an LSTM layer. The hybrid model has proved to outperform all baselines, including a bi-LSTM architecture, with an accuracy of 0.27 on a portion of the dataset used as test.” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 30) (pdf) 此外，最近的一些工作在他们的模型中利用了 RNN 和 CNN 的混合 [2, 87, 101]。 [101] 提出了一种混合方法来检测 LIAR 数据集 [101] 上的假新闻。所提出的架构通过 CNN 对文本信息进行编码，并通过 LSTM 层对文本作者的元数据进行编码。混合模型已证明优于所有基线，包括双 LSTM 架构，在用作测试的部分数据集上的准确度为 0.27。

其他方法

“The approaches discussed in the following tend to exploit models, such as clustering and vector space models, different from the ones presented above in order to identify fake news, rumours and their properties.” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 31) (pdf) 下面讨论的方法倾向于利用与上述不同的模型，例如聚类和向量空间模型，以识别假新闻、谣言及其属性。

“A framework for evaluating tweets and their authors as possible sources of misinformation has been proposed in [55]. In particular, the credibility of a tweet was first evaluated by measuring disparity in retweet behaviour via the Gini coefficient. Credible tweets and their retweets are then used to build a retweet graph. The PageRank algorithm [71] was then used to compute a score of acceptability for the given tweet. Interestingly, such information is then presented to users with the objective of allowing them to perform a more informed evaluation of the tweet.” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 31) (pdf) 在 [55] 中提出了一个评估推文及其作者作为可能的错误信息来源的框架。特别是，首先通过基尼系数测量转发行为的差异来评估推文的可信度。然后使用可信的推文及其转推来构建转推图。然后使用 PageRank 算法 [71] 来计算给定推文的可接受性分数。有趣的是，这些信息随后会呈现给用户，目的是让他们对推文进行更明智的评估。

总结

“The issues discussed in this paper are definitely relevant, from both realworld and research standpoints. The topics of fake news and rumours on the web are relatively new. However, our review of the literature has shown that they are a thriving field of research, and results obtained from both the research and application perspectives are indeed very promising. We expect in fact that previously discussed approaches and techniques will be further improved and will have the potential to be implemented into real-world applications to fight the spread of rumours and fake news online. However, it is clear that improvement is still needed concerning many aspects of the discussed problems.” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 34) (pdf) 从现实世界和研究的角度来看，本文讨论的问题绝对是相关的。网络上的假新闻和谣言话题相对较新。然而，我们对文献的回顾表明，它们是一个蓬勃发展的研究领域，从研究和应用角度获得的结果确实非常有前景。事实上，我们预计之前讨论的方法和技术将得到进一步改进，并有可能被实施到现实世界的应用程序中，以对抗在线谣言和假新闻的传播。然而，很明显，在所讨论问题的许多方面仍需要改进。

“We will now briefly discuss some of the major challenges in fake news and rumours detection and will provide insight on possible future directions for research” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 34) (pdf) 我们现在将简要讨论假新闻和谣言检测中的一些主要挑战，并将就未来可能的研究方向提供见解

“Second, given the streaming nature of social media, information could be subject to concept drift [102]. This means that the relevance of data, as well as proposed features, could vary over time. Both content-based and context-based features, extracted for a given event or time frame, may not scale and generalize well for real-world applications or data from a different context.” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 35) (pdf) 其次，鉴于社交媒体的流媒体性质，信息可能会受到概念漂移的影响[102]。这意味着数据的相关性以及提议的特征可能会随着时间而变化。为给定事件或时间框架提取的基于内容和基于上下文的特征可能无法很好地扩展和概括真实世界的应用程序或来自不同上下文的数据。

“For these reasons, future research in this direction should focus on better understanding the importance of certain features for classification, as well as their ability to generalize on the problem and possibly manage concept drift in a real-world scenario” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 35) (pdf) 由于这些原因，该方向的未来研究应侧重于更好地理解某些特征对分类的重要性，以及它们概括问题并可能在现实世界场景中管理概念漂移的能力

“Given our evaluation of the available literature, we can argue that the most effective approaches implement both content- and context-based features for classification. This has become apparent especially in rumour related research, where we can clearly identify a trend to use as much information as possible for detection. Hybrid approaches, which can simultaneously model different aspects of fake news, such as the actual text, diffusion patterns and stance towards the news, despite being more complex in terms of models, data availability and number of features, may be suited to better solve the problem and could be definitely pursued” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 36) (pdf) 鉴于我们对现有文献的评估，我们可以认为最有效的方法实现了基于内容和基于上下文的分类特征。这在谣言相关研究中变得尤为明显，我们可以清楚地确定使用尽可能多的信息进行检测的趋势。混合方法可以同时对假新闻的不同方面进行建模，例如实际文本、传播模式和对新闻的立场，尽管在模型、数据可用性和特征数量方面更加复杂，但可能适合更好地解决问题，绝对可以追究

“Despite the undoubted importance of tools able to help users of social media, and more generally of the web, there are a few concerning aspects that need to be addressed. First of all, such systems, in the wrong hands, may generate a form of automatic censorship, by means of producing false positives in the search for fake news. Independent sources of news may be targeted because of contrasting viewpoints with mainstream media. Second, news deemed as fake may turn out as real also months, if not years, later. A viable strategy to overcome these potential limitations could be to build real-world applications with such issues in mind. For example, the final decision of whether to trust the piece of news/rumour and the detection system could be left to the end user. Moreover, as earlier research has suggested [15], a continuous score of credibility or trustworthiness could be presented to the user instead of a dichotomous classification between true and fake.” (Bondielli 和 Marcelloni, 2019, p. 37) (pdf) 尽管能够帮助社交媒体用户以及更普遍的网络用户的工具的重要性毋庸置疑，但仍有一些相关方面需要解决。首先，此类系统如果落入坏人之手，可能会通过在搜索假新闻时产生误报来产生一种自动审查形式。由于与主流媒体的观点形成鲜明对比，独立的新闻来源可能会成为攻击目标。其次，被认为是假的新闻可能会在几个月甚至几年后变成真实的。克服这些潜在限制的可行策略可能是在考虑这些问题的情况下构建实际应用程序。例如，是否信任新闻/谣言和检测系统的最终决定可以留给最终用户。此外，正如早期研究表明的那样[15]，可以向用户呈现可信度或可信度的连续分数，而不是真假之间的二分分类。
